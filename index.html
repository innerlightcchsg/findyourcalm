Our Scents

Lavender – A calming scent known for its relaxing properties. It helps reduce stress and anxiety, making it great for unwinding after a long day.

Rose – A floral fragrance that promotes comfort. It is often associated with easing low mood and encouraging calmness and self-love.

Clary Sage – A herbal scent that helps clear your mind and reduce feelings of overwhelm. It is especially useful during times of pressure.

Green Apple – A fresh, crisp fragrance that feels uplifting and energising. It can help improve focus and boost mood, making it very useful for studying.

Jasmine – A gentle, sweet floral scent that promotes relaxation and emotional wellbeing. It reduces stress and creates a peaceful atmosphere.

About Us

InnerLight is a self-care brand created to support teenage mental wellbeing. Our purpose is to provide calming, aesthetic products that help young people relax and manage everyday stress. We are committed to making a positive impact by donating a portion of our profits to YoungMinds, supporting those facing mental health challenges. Through our products and mission, InnerLight aims to create a sense of comfort, awareness, and meaningful change.
